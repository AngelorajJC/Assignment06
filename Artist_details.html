About

Chandrika Silva is a renowned pottery artist from Colombo, Sri Lanka, known for her exquisite handcrafted ceramics. Her work blends traditional techniques with contemporary designs, reflecting the rich cultural heritage of Sri Lanka. Chandrika's passion for pottery began in her childhood, inspired by her grandmother's craft. She trained heavily at the National Craft Council and has since exhibited her work internationally. Each piece is meticulously crafted, showcasing her dedication to excellence in pottery. Chandrika's creations range from functional tableware to decorative art pieces, all bearing her unique touch and artistic vision.
